I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Caltech, advised by Prof. Changhuei Yang. My research explores intelligent optical systems that bring high-throughput, data-driven insight to life sciences via vigorous computational imaging.

  • Computational microscopy for scalable, high-resolution biological discovery
  • Physics-informed machine learning for quantitative imaging and reconstruction
  • Efficient instrumentation that bridges optics, algorithms, and automation

I am currently seeking internship opportunities for Summer 2026 that leverage optical engineering, machine learning, and imaging science.
