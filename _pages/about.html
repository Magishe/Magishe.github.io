I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Caltech, advised by Prof. Changhuei Yang. I build computational microscopy systems that pair novel optical hardware with learning-based reconstruction to capture biological dynamics at scale.

  • Computational microscopy for gigapixel, quantitative imaging of complex specimens
  • Physics-informed machine learning that accelerates microscopy design and automation
  • Hybrid illumination and instrumentation strategies for robust, high-throughput acquisition

My goal is to transition advanced imaging ideas from the lab into impactful tools for life science discovery and translational medicine.

I am currently seeking internship opportunities for Summer 2026.
